Select the default timezone of your organisation

The timezone selected will be displayed wherever time and date is displayed in the appllication.

Select the default language of your organisation

This setting will change the default display language for all pages of the Application and Self Service Portal as well as email notification. You can also define a different language per site as well as per user (via user card). The setting per user takes precedence over the site setting, which itself takes precedence over the organisation-wide setting.

Business hours

This setting is used to enable business hours to adapt your business rules, Service Level Agreement, and reports to your working hours.
